Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access manual controls?
Tap on any parameter (ISO, Shutter, Focus, WB) in the bottom bar to reveal the manual adjustment slider. Tap the "A" icon on any parameter to toggle between auto and manual mode.
Where are my photos saved?
All photos and videos are saved directly to your iPhone's Photo Library. You can access them in the Photos app.
How do I enable RAW capture?
Go to Settings (gear icon) ‚Üí Format and select ProRAW or RAW. Note: ProRAW requires iPhone 12 Pro or later.
What's the difference between ProRAW and RAW?
ProRAW combines Apple's computational photography with RAW data, available up to 48MP on supported devices. Standard RAW (Bayer) is limited to 12MP but provides pure sensor data.
How do I use the histogram?
Enable the histogram in Settings ‚Üí Overlays. It shows the distribution of tones in your image. Tap the histogram to switch between Luminance and RGB modes.
How do I record video?
Swipe left on the mode selector to switch to Video mode. Tap the red record button to start/stop recording. Access video settings in the gear menu.
How do I restore my subscription?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
