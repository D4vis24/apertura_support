Apertura Pro Cam

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2024

Summary: Apertura Pro Cam is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photos and videos remain on your device.

Information We Collect

Apertura Pro Cam does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any identifying information.
  • No usage analytics: We do not track how you use the app.
  • No photo/video access: Your photos and videos are saved directly to your device's photo library and are never uploaded to any server.
  • No location tracking: If you enable geotagging, location data is embedded directly into your photos on-device and is never transmitted to us.

Camera and Photo Library Access

Apertura Pro Cam requires access to:

  • Camera: Required to capture photos and videos. Camera data is processed entirely on your device.
  • Photo Library: Required to save your captured photos and videos. We only write to your library; we do not read or access existing photos.
  • Microphone: Optional, used only for recording audio with video. Audio is processed on-device only.
  • Location: Optional, used only for geotagging photos if you enable this feature. Location data stays on your device.

Subscription Information

Apertura Pro Cam offers optional subscription purchases through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

Third-Party Services

Apertura Pro Cam does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Data Storage

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms. This data is not accessible to us and is not transmitted anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Apertura Pro Cam does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
