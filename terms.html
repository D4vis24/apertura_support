2. Description of Service

Apertura Pro Cam is a professional camera application for iOS that provides:

  • Manual camera controls (ISO, shutter speed, focus, white balance)
  • Multiple shooting modes (Photo, Video, Burst, Long Exposure, Night, Timelapse, AEB)
  • Photo and video editing capabilities
  • LUT (Look-Up Table) filters
  • Professional overlays (histogram, focus peaking, zebras, spirit level)
  • Photo Spots discovery feature for finding photography locations
  • Geotagging capabilities

3. Location Services

Certain features of the App use location services:

Location services are optional. You may use the core camera features without enabling location access. Location data is processed on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties except as required by Apple MapKit for location searches.
